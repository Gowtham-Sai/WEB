About us :

Welcome to GMR Institute of Technology (GMRIT). Established in the year 1997 by GMR Varalakshmi Foundation – the corporate social responsibility arm of GMR Group – GMRIT offers aspiring engineers high quality technical education.
GMRIT
Ranked among the top 100 (government and private Engg) colleges and Ranked 13th among the private institutions in the country. Also ranked 9th in the south region by Data Quest Employability Ranking 2021, September 2021. |Ranked with “AAAAA” rating among the finest private engineering colleges in the South Zone - AP: by Digital Learning, September 2021. | Ranked in the band of 201-250 at National level in NIRF ranking ,MHRD,New Delhi, September 2021.

Located in Rajam, Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh, GMRIT provides its learning community state-of-the-art facilities, infrastructure and a competent faculty. The Institute encourages collaborative learning between industry and academia as a means of reinforcing its curriculum with practical and real world experiences. It is this emphasis on a well-rounded education that makes GMRIT a preferred institute among engineering colleges in India.
